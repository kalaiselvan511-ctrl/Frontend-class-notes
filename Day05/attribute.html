PARIS TOWER

The iconic "Paris tower" is the Eiffel Tower, a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It is one of the most recognizable structures in the world and a global cultural icon of France

The Paris Tower is the Eiffel Tower, a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris. Constructed between 1887 and 1889, it was built as the centerpiece for the 1889 World's Fair, which celebrated the 100th anniversary of the French Revolution.
